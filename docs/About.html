About

Project Origin

This website is an assignment for a graduate level course, IS 601 - Web Systems Development. This course is offered by Professor Keith Williams at New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT).

Project Purpose

The purpose of this website is to gain a better understanding of the history of the internet. Sections of the website include Invention of the Internet, Browser Wars, Internet Search, Dot Com Bubble, People of the Internet, and History of Internet Website. These sections are based of from videos and the powerpoint slides given to us from the assignment. We hope you find this useful in trying to get a better understanding of the history of the internet.
