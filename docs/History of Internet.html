History of Internet Website

Internet Definition

The Internet, a loosely-organized international collaboration of autonomous, interconnected networks, supports host-to-host communication through voluntary adherence to open protocols and procedures defined by Internet Standards.

Technology Preparation

# Time People Event
1 Oct 4, 1957 Russia puts Sputnik into orbit
2 Feb 7, 1958 Space exploration assigned toAdvanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA)
3 Oct 1, 1958 NASA opens for business via Eisenhower executive order
4 July 1961 Leonard Kleinrock of MIT First paper on packet switching theory.
5 August 1962 J.C.R. Licklider of MIT Memos on “Galactic Network Concept”
6 Oct 1969 Larry Roberts Heads project that connects first two ARPANET sites
7 Oct 1972 Bob Kahn Demonstrates ARPANET at the International Computer Communication Conference (ICCC)
